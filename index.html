How to Use a Custom Domain

  1. Buy a domain from Namecheap, GoDaddy, or Google Domains.
  2. Go to your domain's DNS settings.
  3. Add an A Record pointing to your server IP or Vercel domain.
  4. Use CNAME records for subdomains if needed.
  5. Wait 5–30 minutes for propagation. Done!
